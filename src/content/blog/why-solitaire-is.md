## Proven Strategies to Maximize Your Winnings

Winning at cash-prize Solitaire requires more than just luck. Here are some expert tips to give you a competitive edge:

1.  **Practice Makes Perfect**: Before entering cash tournaments, spend time in the free-to-play or practice modes. Get a feel for the app's controls, timing, and scoring system.
2.  **Speed is Key**: Most games are timed, and finishing early grants you a time bonus. Learn to make decisions quickly without sacrificing accuracy.
3.  **Prioritize Revealing Cards**: Your main goal should be to uncover the face-down cards in the tableau. This opens up more potential moves.
4.  **Draw from the Stockpile Wisely**: Only draw from the stockpile when you have no other moves. Cycling through the deck costs time and sometimes points.
5.  **Empty Stacks Strategically**: An empty tableau pile is a powerful asset. Use it to temporarily hold cards and rearrange your columns, but always have a plan for your King.
6.  **Don't Rush to the Foundation**: Moving cards to the foundation is final. Sometimes, it's better to keep low-value cards in the tableau to help you move other cards around.
7.  **Take Advantage of Bonuses**: Log in daily to claim bonuses and use any available promo codes. These can give you free entry into tournaments or extra cash to play with.
